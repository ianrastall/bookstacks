The same and a Gendarme.

GENDARME. An official from St. Petersburg sent by imperial order has arrived, and wants
to see you all at once. He is stopping at the inn.

All are struck as by a thunderbolt. A cry of amazement bursts from the ladies
simultaneously. The whole group suddenly shifts positions and remains standing as if
petrified.
